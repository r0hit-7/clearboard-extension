ClearBoard

YouTube Lecture Note Helper

1
Open a YouTube lecture Navigate to any YouTube video with a classroom or lecture recording.
2
Click 🎓 on the page A sidebar will appear. Optionally capture a "clear board" reference frame.
3
Start scanning Live Watch – detects as you watch in real-time.
Fast Scan – seeks through the whole video automatically.
4
Click any timestamp Jump directly to moments when the board is clear to take notes!

Works on lecture videos where a person stands in front of a blackboard or whiteboard.
